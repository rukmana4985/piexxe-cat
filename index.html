What We Do

We get to know you: your challenges, motivations, and vision.

Our focus is on your customers, because that is the heart of your business. It's imperative that any solution we build together be something your team can take on after our engagement. We are not about the "new shiny", or that buzzword technology everyone is telling you about. Too often, companies will organize and develop processes around 'what is' versus 'what can be.' This often wastes time, frustrates employees, and most of all fails to meet the needs of current and future customers. Fancy technology that fails your customers fails you. Technology and solutions should be based on your customer's needs. It's all about your business.

How We Do It

Our value is in redefining our clients.

We take an iterative approach to product design. Discovery, design, and development work in harmony as your needs and technology evolve. Our process is constantly in motion.

"They are harsh questioners."

- Muhammad Ali Al Khawarizmi

discovery

Discovery is possibly the most critical aspect of the project life cycle. Clearly defining requirements and translating then into a path to victory is a team effort. We ask a lot of questions, even if we think we already know the answers. We go all in which means we often make decisions on your behalf. It's important our team has sufficient understanding of "the why" behind every decision. As your partner, we're not here to tell you what will make a bigger project, we're here to help you discover what will make you more flexible, efficient, and profitable.

There are three major components to every business: your technology, your people, and your customers. We build business systems based in whatever technology helps your people best serve your customer's needs.

"We're completely design aware; it permeates everything we do."

- umar ibnu khattab

DESIGN

Our design team works closely with clients throughout every project to make sure we're driving forward. Working with you to create proper visual guides in the form of journey maps, wireframes, and architectural diagrams drives the project forward and keeps everyone working towards a common goal.

It may sound simple, but it's not. Creating flexible and efficient workflows and architecting reliable business systems can be extremely complicated. We consider ourselves a “design aware” organization. Our methods are integrated into our development process, allowing us to be extremely efficient at creating products and solutions. We start by gathering, prioritizing, and narrowing down requirements. Then we build, review and then iterate until we create the right solution.

"pixxeCat is absolutely committed to mutual success."

DEVELOPMENT

Development isn't just about code. Creating what is needed with consideration for all the people involved is a big part of the productOps way. What we build must last and be maintainable. Otherwise, it fails to provide you or your customers real value. Developing a strong relationship is what allows us to regularly communicate with you, and you know honest communication. If there's bad news, let's talk about it early. If there's good news, let's talk about it often. And, lets make sure the team we collaborate with is prepared to maintain and improve what we have built, in order to provide value to your customers for years to come.

You benefit from the diversity of our employees and our customers. Whether it's moving to the cloud, IoT, or product development, we’ve done it.
